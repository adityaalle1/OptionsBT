Frequently Asked Questions
How does the simulation work?▼
OptionsBT replays real historical OHLCV data bar-by-bar. At each bar, you can open options positions priced with the Black-Scholes-Merton model using the bar's closing price and a calculated implied volatility. When you skip to expiry, the outcome (profit or loss) is calculated from the actual price at expiration.
Where does the price data come from?▼
Daily OHLCV data is fetched from Yahoo Finance via a Cloudflare Worker proxy. Data is cached locally for 24 hours to reduce API calls. Up to 2 years of history is available for US stocks and ETFs.
How is options pricing calculated?▼
Prices use the Black-Scholes-Merton formula. Historical volatility (20-bar) serves as the base IV, adjusted per-strike with a volatility smile. Greeks (delta, gamma, theta, vega) are also computed for each contract.
What fees are included?▼
The default model charges $0.65/contract (matching tastytrade and Schwab), a $19.95 assignment/exercise fee, and the SEC/FINRA regulatory fee ($0.000229 per dollar notional on sell orders). All three are configurable in Trading settings.
How do I save a session?▼
Sessions are saved automatically when you reset the simulation (↺ Reset) or load a new ticker. You can view all saved sessions in the History tab. Sign in to sync them across devices.
How is the win rate benchmark set?▼
Each strategy has a researched benchmark win rate based on typical real-world outcomes (e.g. Wheel ≈ 74%, Long Call ≈ 38%). Your scorecard compares your actual win rate against this target. The benchmark is not a guarantee — it is a reference point for self-assessment.
Why does my score change between sessions?▼
The scorecard grades four components: return vs benchmark, win rate, discipline (fee impact and position sizing), and consistency (max drawdown). Each component is 25 points. Different stocks and market conditions will affect each component differently.
Can I recover a deleted session?▼
Yes — deleted sessions go to the Trash in Data settings and are kept for 30 days before permanent removal. Click Restore to bring a session back.
How do I sync across devices?▼
Sign in with your email. Once signed in, clicking "Sync with cloud" in the account menu (or via the avatar dropdown) will push your local sessions and journal to the cloud and pull any changes from other devices.
Is this real trading? Can I lose money?▼
No. OptionsBT is a paper-trading simulator for education only. No real money is involved and no brokerage is connected. Past simulated performance does not predict real trading results. Options trading in real life carries substantial risk of loss.

Options Strategy Backtester — Real Historical Data
Practice any options strategy on real historical price data. Premiums are priced with Black-Scholes using each stock's own realized volatility, so numbers match real-market behavior.
Data source: Yahoo Finance via CORS proxy. Works for any US stock or ETF. Requires internet connection.
Available Strategies
  • The Wheel — Sell puts → get assigned → sell covered calls
  • Long Call / Long Put — Directional bets with defined risk
  • Cash-Secured Put — Income generation; acquire stock cheaply
  • Covered Call — Generate income on stock you already own
  • Bull Call Spread / Bear Put Spread — Defined-risk directional
  • Iron Condor — Profit from low volatility with 4 legs
How It Works
  • Load any ticker → bars replay historically bar by bar
  • Pause anytime, select a strike, open a position
  • Realistic commissions ($0.65/contract), assignment fees ($19.95), bid-ask spreads
  • Positions expire automatically with full notification
  • All sessions saved to History with full trade log
